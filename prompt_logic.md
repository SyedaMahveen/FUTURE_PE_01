# Prompt Logic Documentation

## Prompt Used

Act as a professional website copywriter and marketing expert.

Create website content for an event management company named "Red Carpet Event Management".

Business Details:

* Industry: Event Management
* Services:

  * Wedding Planning
  * Corporate Events
  * Birthday Parties
  * Stage Decoration
  * Event Coordination

Generate:

1. Homepage Hero Section
2. About Us Section
3. Why Choose Us Section
4. Service Descriptions
5. Contact Call-To-Action

Use professional, persuasive, customer-friendly language suitable for a modern business website.

---

## Prompt Engineering Strategy

* Defined AI Role (Website Copywriter)
* Specified Business Type (Event Management)
* Listed Services Clearly
* Defined Required Sections
* Requested Marketing-Oriented Language
* Requested Website-Ready Output

---

## Expected Outcome

Generate professional website content that can be directly used on a business website and help increase customer engagement and inquiries.

---

## AI Output Summary

The AI successfully generated:

* Homepage Hero Section
* About Us Content
* Services Page Content
* Contact Call-To-Action
* Marketing-Focused Website Copy

The generated content was reviewed and organized into separate deliverable files for easy implementation on a business website.
